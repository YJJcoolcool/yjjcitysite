02 - About our community

Here in YJJ City, we accept all types of people, we are not strict on building rules and you can do pretty much whatever you like as long as it does not harm the server or it's members.

But why do we do this?

This is because we believe that through this freedom of being able to do and create anything, we allow players to unleash their full creativity and invent new things.
