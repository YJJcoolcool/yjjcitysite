02 - About our community

Now, before I introduce you to our community, I want to set one thing straight.

We absolutely DO NOT tolerate bullying.

Whether you think it is fun or cool to bully or harass others, this is absolutely not the place to do so.

Our City is here to welcome and treat others as a family, despite our differences.

All right, I believe most of us here are here to enjoy the experience anyway. So, I won't emphasize further on that.

Here in YJJ City, we have an inviting culture.

We accept and welcome all types of people.

When someone has done something wrong, we Forgive and Forget. Everyone deserves a second chance.

I understand, and know from other City/Building servers, that there are strict regulations when it comes to building. Here in YJJ City, we do it in another way, because let's be honest, nobody likes to see a build they have spent hours building get destroyed because people don't like it.

So how do we do it?

We use an online Building Registration System where Citizens and above (Yes, Citizens can build too!) can apply for registration of a building. They simply fill up a form describing where they would like to claim a plot of land, and what they would like to do with it.

Don't be too worried about your build being accepted or not. If it isn't a 1Ã—1 block tower, or a pile of random blocks, then we would most probably accept it.

Oh remember that part above where I said Citizens can build too? We are also implementing a system where Citizens can buy blocks from Suppliers, and Citizens can also work as Suppliers too!
